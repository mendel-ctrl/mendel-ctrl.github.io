Welcome

Welcome to the CYP S. Petersburg Chai Club! The Chai Club is a passionate and committed group of monthly givers devoted to the continuity of our Jewish community in S. Petersburg. People like YOU, from all walks of life, give anything they can, to prove how great this community can be when you give from the heart.

It's not a subscription, it's an investment.

Why Give Monthly?

Four reasons members say yes

It's An Investment

By giving monthly, you invest what makes sense for you and make an incredible impact over the course of your membership.

It's A Community

Becoming a Chai Club member allows you to join a dedicated group of supporters who understand the importance of making a sustaining commitment to the future of Jewish life in St. Pete.

It's Sustained Support

Consistency matters. For Jewish continuity. For our sustainability. Monthly gifts enable us to invest in specific areas of our work, expand our programs, and innovate because we know we have the ongoing support of the Chai Club community.

It's Exciting

See how your donation is being put to work with updates exclusively for Chai Club members.
