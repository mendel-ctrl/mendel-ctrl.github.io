Who we are

We meet every young Jewish professional exactly where they are — in their career, their questions, and their journey.

Questions?

Still not sure? Just message us — the easiest answer is usually a seat at the next dinner.

I'm new to St. Pete — where do I start?
Message us on WhatsApp or through the contact section below. We'll personally reach out within a day or two and invite you to a Shabbat dinner, a First Friday, or just coffee. That's the best way in.
Do I need to be religious or know anything?
Not at all. CYP is for every young Jewish professional, wherever you are on your journey — no background, affiliation, or knowledge required. Most people show up the first time not knowing a soul, and leave with friends.
Who actually runs CYP St. Pete?
Rabbi Mendel and Chaya Lebovics — a rabbi and rebbetzin who live in St. Petersburg full-time. They aren't coordinators flying in; they're your neighbors. Show up to one Shabbat dinner and you'll see what we mean.
How much does any of this cost?
Coming to dinners and events is always free and open — we never want cost to be the reason someone doesn't come. If you'd like to support the community and help it grow, that's what the Chai Club is for, and it's entirely optional.
